Song Idea & Facts

  • Most songs follow a structure: Verse–Chorus–Verse–Chorus–Bridge–Chorus.
  • Lyrics often reflect emotions or tell a story.
  • Melody and harmony should complement the lyrical theme.
  • ⚠️ Always confirm songwriting techniques with a teacher or professional resource.
