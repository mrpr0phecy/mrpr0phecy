Cable Length Results & Facts

  • Voltage drop is calculated as ΔV = (2 × I × L × ρ) ÷ A.
  • ρ (resistivity) ≈ 0.018 Ω·mm²/m for copper, ≈ 0.029 for aluminium.
  • Maximum voltage drop is typically limited to 3–5% by standards.
  • Longer runs require larger conductor sizes to stay within limits.
  • ⚠️ Always verify cable sizing with local code tables and a licensed electrician.
