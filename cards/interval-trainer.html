Interval Trainer (General Advice)

Practice identifying musical intervals. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify with a teacher or professional resource.

Interval Output & Facts

  • Intervals measure the distance between two notes in semitones.
  • Major third = 4 semitones, minor third = 3 semitones.
  • Perfect fifth = 7 semitones, octave = 12 semitones.
  • ⚠️ Always confirm interval recognition with a teacher or professional resource.
