Motor Results & Facts

  • Motors draw high inrush current at startup, often 5–7× running current.
  • Starting method (DOL, star‑delta, soft starter, VFD) affects inrush magnitude.
  • Running current depends on power, voltage, efficiency, and power factor.
  • Excessive voltage dip during startup can damage equipment or trip breakers.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed electrician before installation.
