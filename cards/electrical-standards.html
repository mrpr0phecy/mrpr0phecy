Electrical Standards Reference (General Advice)

Overview of common electrical standards. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify with local regulations and a licensed professional.

Standards Reference & Facts

  • IEC (International Electrotechnical Commission) publishes global standards for electrical safety and performance.
  • NEC (National Electrical Code, USA) governs wiring, protection, and installation practices.
  • BS 7671 (UK Wiring Regulations) sets requirements for electrical installations in the UK.
  • Local codes may modify or add to international standards.
  • ⚠️ Always consult the latest official code and a licensed electrician.
