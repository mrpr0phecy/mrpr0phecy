Transformer Results & Facts

  • Transformer size is usually expressed in kVA.
  • Formula (three‑phase): kVA = (√3 × V × I) / 1000.
  • Formula (single‑phase): kVA = (V × I) / 1000.
  • Efficiency and power factor affect actual load demand.
  • Always add margin for future expansion and safety.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed engineer before installation.
