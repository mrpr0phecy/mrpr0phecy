Materials Science Results & Facts

  • Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon.
  • Diamond is the hardest natural material (Mohs 10).
  • Graphene is stronger than steel yet flexible.
  • Shape‑memory alloys return to shape when heated.
  • Concrete is the most widely used man‑made material.

Environmental Results & Facts

  • Trees absorb ~22 kg CO₂ per year.
  • Recycling one aluminum can saves 3 hours of TV energy.
  • Freshwater is only ~2.5% of Earth’s water.
  • Plastic can take centuries to decompose.
  • Renewables reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
