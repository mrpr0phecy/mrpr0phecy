Breaker Results & Facts

  • Breakers protect conductors from overcurrent.
  • Continuous loads are often calculated at 125% of rated current.
  • Breaker size must never exceed conductor ampacity.
  • Standard breaker ratings are typically 10A, 16A, 20A, 32A, etc.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed electrician before installation.
