Music Theory Output & Facts

  • Scales are sequences of notes arranged by pitch (major, minor, modes).
  • Intervals measure the distance between two notes (e.g. major third, perfect fifth).
  • Chords are built from stacking intervals, usually thirds.
  • Keys define the tonal center of a piece.
  • ⚠️ Always confirm theory concepts with a teacher or professional resource.
