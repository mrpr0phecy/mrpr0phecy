Instrument Tuner (General Advice)

Tune your instrument by matching pitch. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify tuning with your ear or a professional device.

Tuner Output & Facts

  • Standard guitar tuning is EADGBE.
  • Bass guitars typically use EADG.
  • Violin tuning is GDAE, ukulele is GCEA.
  • ⚠️ Always fine‑tune by ear or with a professional tuner for accuracy.
