Solar Results & Facts

  • Solar panel sizing depends on daily load, sun hours, and panel rating.
  • Formula: Panels = (Daily Load Wh) / (Panel W × Sun Hours × Efficiency).
  • System efficiency accounts for inverter losses, wiring, and temperature effects.
  • Always add a margin for cloudy days and seasonal variation.
  • ⚠️ Always verify solar designs with local installers and standards.
