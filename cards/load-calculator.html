Load Calculator (General Advice)

Estimate total electrical load from appliances. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify with a qualified electrician and local standards.

Load Results & Facts

  • Total load is the sum of appliance power ratings.
  • Diversity factor accounts for not all appliances running simultaneously.
  • Continuous loads are often calculated at 125% of rated current.
  • Load calculations guide circuit sizing but must be checked against local codes.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed electrician before installation.
