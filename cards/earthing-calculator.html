Earthing Results & Facts

  • Earthing conductors must withstand fault current without damage.
  • Formula: S = I × √t ÷ k, where S = cross‑sectional area (mm²).
  • k depends on material and permissible temperature rise (≈115 for copper, ≈76 for aluminium).
  • Fault duration is usually based on protective device clearing time.
