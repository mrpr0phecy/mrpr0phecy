Capo Output & Facts

  • A capo raises the pitch of open strings by semitones.
  • Placing capo on fret 2 raises the key by a whole step.
  • Capos allow familiar chord shapes to be used in new keys.
  • ⚠️ Always confirm capo placement with a teacher or professional resource.
