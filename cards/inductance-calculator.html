Inductance Results & Facts

  • Inductance depends on turns, core area, length, and permeability.
  • Formula: L = (µ₀ × µr × N² × A) / l.
  • µ₀ (vacuum permeability) ≈ 4π × 10⁻⁷ H/m.
  • High permeability cores increase inductance.
  • ⚠️ Always verify coil design with datasheets and standards.
