Battery Results & Facts

  • Battery capacity is measured in amp‑hours (Ah).
  • Formula: Ah = (Load W × Runtime h) / (Voltage V × Efficiency).
  • Always add a safety margin (e.g. 20%) for aging and discharge limits.
  • Deep‑cycle batteries are recommended for long runtimes.
  • ⚠️ Always verify battery sizing with manufacturer datasheets and local standards.
