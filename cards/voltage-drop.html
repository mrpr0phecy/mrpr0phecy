Voltage Drop Results & Facts

  • Voltage drop should be kept under 3% for branch circuits, 5% overall.
  • Longer runs and higher currents increase voltage drop.
  • Copper has lower resistance than aluminum, reducing voltage drop.
  • Excessive voltage drop can cause equipment malfunction or overheating.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed electrician before installation.
