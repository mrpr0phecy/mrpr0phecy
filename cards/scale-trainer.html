Scale Trainer (General Advice)

Practice scales in different keys. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify with a music teacher or professional resource.

Scale Results & Facts

  • Major scale formula: W–W–H–W–W–W–H (whole/half steps).
  • Natural minor scale formula: W–H–W–W–H–W–W.
  • Pentatonic scales use 5 notes; blues adds a flat 5th.
  • ⚠️ Always confirm scale notes with a teacher or professional resource.
