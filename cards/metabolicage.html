How it works

BMR (Mifflin–St Jeor):
• Male: 10×W + 6.25×H − 5×A + 5
• Female: 10×W + 6.25×H − 5×A − 161
We estimate metabolic age by mapping your BMR against typical age‑group averages (higher BMR → younger estimate).
