Capacitor Results & Facts

  • Capacitors store energy in an electric field.
  • RC cutoff frequency is f = 1 / (2πRC).
  • Capacitance is measured in farads (F); practical values are often µF or nF.
  • Voltage rating must exceed circuit voltage.
  • ⚠️ Always verify capacitor selection with datasheets and standards.
