Ear Trainer (General Advice)

Practice recognizing intervals and pitches. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify with a music teacher or professional resource.

Ear Training Output & Facts

  • Ear training improves recognition of intervals, chords, and melodies.
  • Major third sounds “bright,” minor third sounds “sad.”
  • Perfect fifth is very consonant; octave is the same pitch class higher.
  • ⚠️ Always confirm interval recognition with a teacher or professional resource.
