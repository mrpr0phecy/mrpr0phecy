PCB Trace Results & Facts

  • PCB trace width depends on current, copper thickness, and allowable temperature rise.
  • IPC‑2221 provides industry guidelines for trace sizing.
  • Thicker copper layers allow narrower traces for the same current.
  • Longer traces increase resistance and voltage drop.
  • ⚠️ Always verify PCB designs with IPC standards and manufacturer capabilities.
