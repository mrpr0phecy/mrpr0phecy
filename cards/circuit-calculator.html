Circuit Results & Facts

  • Circuits are sized to carry load current plus a safety margin.
  • Continuous loads are often calculated at 125% of rated current.
  • Voltage drop should be kept under 3% for branch circuits, 5% overall.
  • Breaker size must never exceed conductor ampacity.
  • Always follow local electrical codes (NEC, IEC, BS 7671, etc.).
  • Consult a licensed electrician before installation.
