Wire Gauge Results & Facts

  • Voltage drop should be kept under 3% for branch circuits, 5% overall.
  • Copper has lower resistance than aluminum, so smaller sizes can carry the same current.
  • Longer runs require larger conductors to limit voltage drop.
  • Always match wire size to breaker rating and local code tables.
  • Temperature and bundling affect conductor ampacity — derating may be required.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed electrician before installation.
