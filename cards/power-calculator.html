Power Results & Facts

  • Real power (W) = Voltage × Current × Power Factor.
  • Apparent power (VA) = Voltage × Current.
  • Three‑phase systems use √3 × V × I × PF for real power.
  • Continuous loads are often calculated at 125% of rated current.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed electrician before installation.
