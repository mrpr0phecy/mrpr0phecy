Estimate inverter size for a given load. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify with a qualified installer and local standards.

Inverter Results & Facts

  • Inverter size is usually expressed in VA or kVA.
  • Formula: Inverter VA = Load W ÷ Efficiency.
  • Always account for surge loads (motors, compressors, pumps).
  • System voltage determines DC current drawn from batteries.
  • ⚠️ Always consult a licensed installer before installation.
