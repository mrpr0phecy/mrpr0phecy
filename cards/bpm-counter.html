BPM Counter (General Advice)

Count beats per minute by tapping. ⚠️ Educational only — always verify with a teacher or professional device.

BPM Output & Facts

  • BPM = beats per minute, a measure of tempo.
  • Pop songs often range 100–130 BPM; ballads are slower, dance tracks faster.
  • Tap tempo counters are useful for matching unknown song speeds.
  • ⚠️ Always confirm BPM with a professional metronome or teacher.
