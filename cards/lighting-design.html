Lighting Results & Facts

  • Lighting design uses the lumen method: Lamps = (Area × Lux) ÷ (Lumens × UF × MF).
  • Utilization factor accounts for room reflectance and fixture efficiency.
  • Maintenance factor accounts for lamp aging and dirt accumulation.
  • Target illuminance varies: offices ~300 lux, kitchens ~500 lux, living rooms ~150 lux.
  • ⚠️ Always verify lighting design with local standards and professional guidance.
