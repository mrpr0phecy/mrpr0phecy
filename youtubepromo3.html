From Sons of South to MrProphecy – A Continuum of UK Innovation

Sons of South began as a southern UK collective – three MCs, a DJ, a beatmaker and a live band – carving out space in the mid‑2000s underground. Mixtapes like “All Eyes on the South” and “Real Name No Gimmicks” captured their ethos: no industry gloss, no shortcuts, just truth, craft and community.

Out of that crucible came MrProphecy – an artist who took the Sons of South DNA and pushed it into new territory: UK hip hop, UKG, photomotion animation and mythic visual storytelling.
